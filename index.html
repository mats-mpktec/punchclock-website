What is Punch Clock?

Punch Clock is a simple work hours tracker and punch clock app for iPhone. It helps you clock in and out, track breaks, keep a private work time log, and export PDF timesheets when you need a report.

When you only need to know how much you worked, you should not need a full HR platform. Punch Clock is built for hourly workers, consultants, freelancers, small business owners, flexible schedules, and anyone who wants a private record of their work time.

FAQ

Common questions about Punch Clock.

How do I register work time?

Open Punch Clock and clock in when you start working. Clock out when the shift ends. The app tracks the active shift and summarizes your work time.

Can I edit a work shift?

Yes. You can edit start time, end time, breaks, and notes after a shift has been recorded.

Can I export a timesheet?

Yes. You can export PDF reports using preset periods or a custom date range, with summary and detailed report options.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. Punch Clock stores your work shifts, breaks, notes, and edits locally on your device. Data leaves the device only when you export a report or send feedback.

Is Punch Clock a payroll or compliance system?

No. Punch Clock helps you document work time, but it is not legal advice, a payroll service, or a guarantee of compliance. You or your employer are responsible for checking applicable rules.
